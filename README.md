# ⚔️ Bit Dungeon: Durability Patch 🛡️

A minimalist, retro-inspired **Roguelike** built entirely in a single HTML file. Navigate procedurally generated caves and labyrinths, manage your equipment's durability, and survive until Floor 10.

### 🕹️ Features
*   **Dynamic Environments:** Randomly switches between **Open Caverns** and **Tight Mazes**.
*   **Tactical Combat:** Bare-fisted brawling or weapon-based combat.
*   **Durability System:** Your Sword and Shield break over time. Use them wisely!
*   **Progression:** Level up your stats (HP, ATK, DEF) by defeating monsters.
*   **The Merchant:** Encounter the mysterious traveler on Floor 5 to restock your gear.

### 📜 How to Survive
1.  **Don't Waste Your Blade:** Use bare fists for weak bugs (Level 1). Save the sword for wolves and skeletons.
2.  **Mind the Light:** In Maze floors, you are blind without a torch. Keep an eye on your `TRC` (Torch Durability).
3.  **Gold is Life:** Every coin counts. You'll need at least 5 coins to buy a Potion or a fresh Shield at the shop.
4.  **Level Up:** You heal fully when you level up. Time your XP gains to save a Potion!
